About us

LinkedIn Local is a global meetup community that brings online LinkedIn connections to offline meetups so as to take online professional relationships offline to get to know the person behind the profile.

LinkedIn Local - NIT Jalandhar conducts meetup events where speakers experienced from diverse industries and technologies are invited so as to share there views and experiences for the need of professional platforms like LinkedIn.

Why LinkedIn Local ?

Corporate Insights

The student community of NITJ lacks the knowledge of the corporate world and that's where LinkedIn Local becomes the need of the hour. Our speakers are form diverse positions from diverse industries/companies.

How to practically follow your dreams ?

Students are always advised to follow their dreams but nobody teaches them how ? Professional apps like LinkedIn is the way to pursue your dream career.

A sea of opportunities

Meetups results in expansion of your personal professional network. Its been proven that the most connected people are often the most successful.
